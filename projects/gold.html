What exactly is it?

Technology stops for no man or woman. I've found that part of being a web developer inherently involves dabbling in new things, taking what you know from solution A and seeing if solution B needs something else.

A few years back I was starting to write some of my thoughts on various technologies down. This could have just been a list of notes on my computer some where, but I also had some time and interest in getting my hands dirty in a few new (to me) technologies.

More recently I found myself in a similar place, wanting to have a project where I could dive into React. So my note keeper project got a whole new iteration. After some more intentional rebranding and design, I completely started fresh on the code:
